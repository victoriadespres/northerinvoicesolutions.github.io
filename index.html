About Northern Invoice Solutions

Invoice support that understands energy, industrial and construction workflows.

Northern Invoice Solutions was founded by Victoria Despres in Grande Prairie, Alberta to help businesses reduce administrative workload, improve invoice accuracy and keep billing moving.

With hands-on experience processing high-volume invoices across platforms such as OpenInvoice, Ariba, Pandell, Coupa and Energy Payables, Victoria brings practical support to companies that need reliable AP assistance without hiring and training in-house.
